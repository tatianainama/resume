Voices of Merseyside March 2020 - June 2020
github.com/tatianainama/voices-of-merseyside

Voices of Merseyside is a project for graduate thesis research that explores the accents, dialects and indentities of people of the Merseyside region.

The project consisted of two applications: a multiplatform survey and an admin panel for the researcher to analyze and read the results.

The multiplatform survey consisted of a couple of questions followed up of a drawing tool to create shapes around the different areas of Merseyside and assign requested data about those places.

The admin panel performed statistical analysis on the data and allowed the user to export results in a friendly format, along with visualizing multiple properties on heatmaps for more intuitive analysis of the data.
